# Article Title: One-line clear task

**TL;DR:** One-sentence summary of outcome.

## Who is this for
- New Balanze users setting up their first account
- Users wanting to track their financial transactions
- Anyone looking to understand Balanze's core features

## Prerequisites
- Active Balanze account
- Basic understanding of personal finance concepts

## Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account
- Screenshot: `/static/help/step1.png` (Screenshot showing account creation form)
- Navigate to the Accounts section
- Click "Add Account" button
- Fill in account details (name, type, currency, initial balance)

### Step 2: Add Your First Transaction
- GIF: `/static/placeholders/onboarding-gifs/step2.gif` (GIF showing transaction creation)
- Go to Transactions or use the floating action button
- Select transaction type (income/expense)
- Enter amount, category, and description
- Save the transaction

### Step 3: Explore Analytics
- Screenshot: `/static/help/step3.png` (Screenshot of analytics dashboard)
- Visit the Analytics section
- Review your spending patterns
- Explore different chart views and insights

## Expected Result

After completing these steps, you should see:
- Your account listed in the Accounts overview
- Your transaction appearing in the recent transactions list
- Updated balance reflecting your transaction
- Basic analytics showing your financial activity

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Account creation fails
**Fix:** Check that all required fields are filled and you haven't exceeded your account limit

### Problem: Transaction not appearing
**Fix:** Refresh the page or check that the correct account was selected

### Problem: Analytics showing no data
**Fix:** Ensure you have at least one account and transaction created
